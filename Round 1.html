Robo-Wrestling

Knockout Round

1. Qualifier round will be held prior to the knockout round. The duration of the qualifying round will be 2 minutes.


2. In each match, two teams will compete against each other at a time and try to earn maximum points.

3. The teams will be ranked according to their points.


4. Top 16 teams will qualify for the finals. Other than this, the performance in the qualifier round is of no significance to the contest.
