Robo-Wrestling

Hand Touch

1.No hand touches are allowed in between the matches unless both the operators of the respective robots want a restart.


2.If a restart is taken then both the robots will be placed back in their respective starting zones.


3.Even if a participant takes a hand touch, the clock will keep running and will not be reset as the robots start again from the starting points.
